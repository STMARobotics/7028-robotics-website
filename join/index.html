Join the Team

Binary Battalion 7028 is open to students who love solving problems, building together, and creating impact in our community.

Who Can Join?

Students in grades 9–12 from St. Michael-Albertville and surrounding communities are welcome. No experience required — we will teach you!

  • Mechanical design & fabrication
  • Programming & controls
  • Business, outreach, & media

How to Get Started

  1. Attend an info night in the fall or reach out to schedule a tour of our build space.
  2. Complete the team interest form and FIRST registration.
  3. Meet with subteam leads to learn where your skills and interests fit.
