Robots

Season after season we prototype, design, and code machines that reflect our team values: curiosity, grit, and reliability.

2024 — Atlas

Atlas is a swerve-drive robot engineered for precise positioning and rapid cycling. Dual intake rollers feed a telescoping arm capable of scoring in every grid tier.

  • Subsystems: autonomous vision, articulated arm, dual-stage intake
  • Programming highlights: AprilTag localization, adaptive path planning
  • Notable award: Engineering Inspiration (Lake Superior Regional)

2023 — Nova

Nova featured a tank drive base with a focus on consistency. A pneumatic elevator and pivoting cube manipulator provided dependable scoring throughout the season.

  • Subsystems: dual-speed gearbox, elevator, cube claw
  • Programming highlights: auto balance routine, driver assist macros
  • Notable award: Innovation in Control (Minnesota State Championship)
